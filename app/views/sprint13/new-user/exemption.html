Guidance on exemption
Exemptions from training modules will only be considered if you can demonstrate that you have the required knowledge and levels of competence. Typically, you will have already held a personal licence within the previous 5 years that gave you the authority to use animals of the same species, and to perform one or more of the same techniques in the same categories, to those detailed in this application.
